How It's Made

Spring rolls are made by wrapping a mixture of vegetables, meat, or shrimp in thin flour wrappers and deep-frying them until crispy.

Ingredients

  • Flour wrappers
  • Vegetables (cabbage, carrots)
  • Minced meat or shrimp
  • Soy sauce, ginger, garlic

Importance

A favorite in Asian cuisine, spring rolls are light, crunchy, and flavorful, often served with dipping sauces.

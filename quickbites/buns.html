How It's Made

Buns are made by mixing flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and baking powder into a thick batter. This is scooped and deep-fried until golden brown and firm on the outside but soft inside.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk or water

Importance

Buns are a denser alternative to Puff Puff, loved for their subtle sweetness and chewy texture. They are ideal for breakfast and parties.

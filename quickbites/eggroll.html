How It's Made

Egg rolls are prepared by wrapping a boiled egg in soft dough made from flour and seasonings. The dough-covered egg is deep-fried until golden and crisp on the outside.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Boiled eggs
  • Sugar and salt
  • Butter
  • Baking powder

Importance

Egg Roll is a filling and protein-rich snack commonly sold by street vendors in West Africa. It's ideal for breakfast or quick hunger relief.

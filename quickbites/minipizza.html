How It's Made

Mini pizzas are made from a flour dough base, rolled out into small rounds, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, and meats. They are then baked until crusty and bubbling.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegetables and sausage (optional)

How It's Made

Croissants are made from laminated dough, where layers of butter and dough are folded together multiple times. They're rolled into crescent shapes and baked until flaky and golden.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Importance

A staple of French pastry, croissants are enjoyed with coffee for breakfast or used for sandwiches. Their rich buttery layers make them a luxurious treat.

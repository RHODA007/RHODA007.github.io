How It's Made

Meat pies are made by preparing a savory pastry dough and filling it with a mixture of minced meat, onions, potatoes, carrots, and spices. The dough is rolled out, filled, folded, sealed, and baked until golden brown.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Minced meat
  • Onions, carrots, and potatoes
  • Butter or margarine
  • Salt and seasoning

Importance

Meat pies are a beloved snack across many cultures, especially in Nigeria and the UK. They are great for parties, lunch packs, and quick bites. They offer a perfect balance of carbohydrates and proteins in one flaky, savory bite.

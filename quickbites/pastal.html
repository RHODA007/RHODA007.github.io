How It's Made

Nigerian Pastel is made by filling seasoned minced meat or vegetables in dough made from flour, margarine, and water. It’s then deep-fried until crisp and golden.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Minced meat or veggies
  • Seasoning and spices
  • Butter or margarine

Importance

Pastel is a festive favorite in Nigeria and is often compared to meat pie but with a thinner, crisper shell. It’s tasty, filling, and easy to pack.

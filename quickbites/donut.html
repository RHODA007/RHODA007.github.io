How It's Made

Donuts are made from a yeast-based or cake batter dough, rolled out and cut into rings. After rising, they are deep-fried to golden brown perfection and often glazed, sugared, or filled with jam or cream.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter

Importance

Donuts are popular worldwide and serve as a delicious snack or breakfast. Loved for their sweetness and soft, airy texture, they come in endless varieties and bring joy to all age groups.

How It's Made

Fish rolls are made by preparing a spicy fish filling (often sardines with onions, pepper, and tomato) and wrapping it in pastry dough. They are then deep-fried or baked until crisp and golden.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sardines or mackerel
  • Onions, pepper, and seasoning
  • Butter
  • Salt and baking powder

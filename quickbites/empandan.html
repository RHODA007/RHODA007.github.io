How It's Made

Empanadas are made by filling dough circles with a mixture of seasoned meat, cheese, or vegetables. The dough is folded, sealed, and either baked or fried until crisp.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Meat or cheese filling
  • Spices and onions
  • Butter or oil

Importance

A Latin American classic, empanadas are flavorful, portable snacks or meals, perfect for lunch boxes and street food vendors.

How It's Made

Crepes are thin pancakes made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and butter. They're cooked on a flat pan and can be filled with sweet toppings like fruits, Nutella, or savory fillings like cheese and ham.

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

How It's Made

Puff Puff is made by mixing flour, sugar, yeast, and water into a thick batter. After rising, spoonfuls are scooped and deep-fried until golden brown and fluffy inside.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Nutmeg (optional)
  • Water

Importance

A classic West African street snack, Puff Puff is known for its soft texture and sweet taste. It's a staple at parties, gatherings, and celebrations.

How It's Made

Burgers are made by grilling or frying seasoned beef patties, then sandwiching them between flour buns with lettuce, cheese, and condiments. Fries are thinly sliced potatoes fried until crisp.

Ingredients

  • Flour buns
  • Beef patties
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese
  • Potatoes (for fries)

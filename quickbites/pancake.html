How It's Made

Pancakes are made from a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and butter. The mixture is poured into a pan and cooked on both sides until golden. They’re often served with honey or syrup.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar

Importance

Pancakes are a breakfast favorite in many cultures. They’re versatile, easy to make, and go well with both sweet and savory toppings.

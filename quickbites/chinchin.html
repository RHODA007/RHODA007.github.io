How It's Made

Chin Chin is made by mixing flour, sugar, milk, butter, and a pinch of nutmeg. The dough is rolled out, cut into small squares or strips, and deep-fried until golden and crunchy.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Nutmeg
  • Milk or water

How It's Made

Sausage rolls are made by wrapping seasoned sausage meat in a sheet of puff pastry, then baking it until golden and crisp.

Ingredients

  • Flour (for pastry)
  • Sausage meat
  • Onion and spices
  • Egg (for glazing)

Importance

Sausage rolls are popular in bakeries and homes, known for their hearty filling and crisp crust. They’re often served at events or sold as snacks.
